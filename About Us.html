About Us

"Welcome to Hariom Saini Ice Cream Parlour, where delightful flavors meet a tradition of excellence. Founded with a passion for crafting premium quality ice creams, our parlour has been serving a diverse range of flavors to satisfy every palate since 2011. At Hariom Saini, we take pride in using only the finest, freshest ingredients sourced locally to create our creamy concoctions. Our commitment to quality extends beyond taste, as we prioritize hygiene and customer satisfaction in every scoop. What sets us apart is our dedication to innovation, constantly experimenting to introduce new and exciting flavors that surprise and delight our customers. Whether it's the classics or our unique creations, each scoop tells a story of our dedication to the art of ice cream making. Beyond serving delicious treats, we believe in fostering a warm, welcoming environment for our customers. Our friendly staff is here to make your experience memorable, ensuring you leave with a smile on your face and the taste of our delectable ice creams lingering in your memory. Join us at Hariom Saini Ice Cream Parlour to indulge in a world of flavors that tantalize your taste buds and create cherished moments with every scoop."
